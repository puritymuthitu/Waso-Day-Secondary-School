The department comprises Three subjects, namely;

  • Biology
  • Chemistry
  • Physics

There are seven teachers in the science department recruited by the Teachers Services Commission, with two Biology, three teaching Chemistry, and two teaching Physics.

In junior classes all the three sciences are compulsory. In senior classes opt for tripple science (the three sciences) or double science (Biology and Chemistry or Chemistry and Physics) depending on their future career.
